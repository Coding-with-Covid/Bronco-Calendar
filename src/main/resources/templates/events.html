General Meeting - Cal Poly Society of Accountants

Society of Accountants, Cal Poly

Cal Poly Society of Accountants is a professional and independent organization on campus focusing on exposing students to academic and career issues in the accounting profession. Our members are provided with many opportunities to develop essential skills that employers seek. Opportunities for students at Cal Poly Pomona to improve their network with fellow students, professors, and Accounting professionals through weekly meetings, social and community service events, professional development meetings, and professional events such as Mock Interviews and Meet the Firms.We strongly encourage students who are dedicated to self-development and goal setting to join and experience what it has to offer.

Mindfulness Mondays via Zoom

César E. Chávez Center for Higher Education

Enjoy 30 minutes of self-care to start your week calm, focused, and aware. Sessions include meditation, journaling, and other forms of mindfulness. All session will be facilitated by a CAPS Counselor. For more information contact Wendy at wecordova@cpp.edu. In response to CONVID-19, we are now offering this program virtually. In order for everyone to engage in this program we ask the following: Please be in a quiet and relaxing spaceUse a headset/headphonesMute your microphone during the practiceShare your videoBe respectful of others privacy, do not take pictures or record the practiceUse the group chat to express your questionsPlay the music link provided by facilitator (day of) and enjoy Mindfulness Monday!OSLCC guidelines:Please use inclusive language and stray away from inappropriate conversations Be mindful of others' emotional boundaries; everyone is experiencing this time in different ways Cyberbullying will not be tolerated! Respect people's privacy - No one should screen record and/or take pictures without everyone's consent Come with an open heart and open mind Please note the CPP Student Code of Conduct extends to students regardless of physical space or virtual venue, including campus related/non-related events or activities that occur on or off campus.
